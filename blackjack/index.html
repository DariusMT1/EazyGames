How to Play Blackjack

Blackjack is a popular card game played in casinos. The goal of the game is to beat the dealer's hand without going over 21.

Rules:

  • Each player starts with two cards, and the dealer also gets two cards, with one card face up and one face down.
  • Cards 2 through 10 are worth their face value. Face cards (J, Q, K) are worth 10 points. Aces can be worth 1 or 11 points.
  • Players can choose to "Hit" to take another card or "Stand" to keep their current hand.
  • If a player's hand exceeds 21 points, they "bust" and lose the game.
  • After all players have finished their turns, the dealer reveals their face-down card and must hit until their total is 17 or higher.
  • If the dealer busts, all remaining players win. If the dealer does not bust, the player with the highest hand value that does not exceed 21 wins.
  • If the player and dealer have the same hand value, it is a "push" and the player gets their bet back.

How to Play:

  • Click "Hit" to take another card.
  • Click "Stand" to keep your current hand and end your turn.
  • Click "Restart" to start a new game.
